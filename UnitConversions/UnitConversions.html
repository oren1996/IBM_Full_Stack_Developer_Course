Temperature Conversion



Note: To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in Celsius: (C x 9/5) + 32

Weight



Note: To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilograms: kg x 2.205

Distance



Note: To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometers: km ÷ 1.609
